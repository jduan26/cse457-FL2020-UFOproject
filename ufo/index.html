Welcome to UFO, a site dedicated to charting out 85 years worth of UFO sighting data in the United States.

To begin, select a year on our scrollable timeline (we suggest starting somewhere like 1950). The map will then display all UFO sightings in the US for that year.

You can filter the UFO sightings displayed by their reported shape by toggling the buttons in the filter by shape section.

Lastly, you can select a state on the map to see that state's total UFO sighting data across each decade in the chart below.
